## Introduction

Athenna is the plug & play Node.js framework that focuses
on giving a very reliable foundation for the developer to
build many types of different applications, perfect for
working with microservices architecture. Expect an
enjoyable, beautiful, peaceful and productive experience
when working with Athenna.
