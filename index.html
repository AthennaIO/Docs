Why Athenna ?

Athenna is a Node.js framework that focus in many types of application development, such as Http servers, workers, CLI's and more! Athenna lets you focus in the development of your business rules, no more configuring project. Just scaffold the project with our athenna new and everything is working! With Athenna you can build more than a simple Http server, here your can build CLI's for NPM, Http servers and in the future, Workers, GRPc and GraphQL applications! What are you waiting for? hit Get Started button and go check the documentation!
